About Us

The Founders

Emma and Dana are cousins and both passionate about nature and beauty. Emma first began to dig about natural and organic beauty products and pass her passion to her cousin Dana.

Positive, sensitive and entrepreneurs, they are both addict to natural products and decided to create NaturAddict, a brand that offers truly beneficial beauty products to everyone at reasonable prices.

Dana lives in London and develop the business and sell the products while Emma, based in Valence, create the receipes and "confectionne" the products.
